# Use the Remotion Chatbot

The Chatbot answers simple questions about Remotion using CrawlChat-indexed documentation.

## Ways to Invoke

- Click **"Ask AI"** anywhere on Remotion docs
- Visit [remotion.ai](https://remotion.ai)
- Press **⌘/Ctrl + I** while in the Studio

For complex questions, ask on [Discord](https://remotion.dev/discord).
