# Course 3: Fundamentals of Operations with Goldman Sachs 10,000 Women

## Module 1: Fundamentals of Operations

### Welcome (Video)

Transcript:

As a business owner, you already know how important it is to deliver the value that you promise to your customers. Potential new customers are looking for maximum value at the greatest convenience. Well-refined processes that consistently deliver on your brand promise can give you a competitive edge over your competition. They can also influence your business's reputation. Customer frustration or delight related to key areas, such as delivery, often have a significant impact on the reviews shared on the Internet. This course focuses on your business's operations. This is a broad term, which refers to all of the activity that happens within your company to keep it running smoothly, and to deliver on your customer promise.

Operational processes are structured collections of the tasks (or steps) required to produce the product or service offered to your customers. As your business grows, it is common to find that the operational processes, which previously worked well, become strained as they scale to meet increasing customer demands or a pivot in strategy. You will explore how refining your operational processes can deliver: efficiency for growth, value for customers, and competitive advantage. You will also identify key processes in your business that require further development. In addition, you will explore practical steps to ensure you invest your time and resources in the processes that are most important to your business and target customers. Now it is time to get started, and explore the operations in your business.
